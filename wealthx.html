Accounts: Setup and Daily Maintenance

WealthX keeps account structure visible from the first screen, then lets you edit balances, swipe for quick actions, and manage grouped accounts without leaving the flow.

  • Create account groups or standalone accounts from the same entry point.
  • Update balances and notes directly inside account detail.
  • Use swipe actions and link management to keep the structure clean over time.

Budgets + Goals: Planning That Adapts

Version 2.1 combines classic budget management with Smart Budget suggestions and a dedicated Goal Tracker so planning and progress live together.

  • Manage monthly, yearly, and project budgets from one workspace.
  • Review Smart Budget suggestions before adjusting or archiving budget items.
  • Track saving goals with target amount, due date, linked accounts, and progress detail.

Bookkeeping: Normal, Transfer, Recurring, Refund

Daily bookkeeping stays flexible across standard transactions, transfers, recurring entries, and refund or return handling.

  • Start from the Today screen and move between transaction types without leaving the entry flow.
  • Recurring logic helps repeated bills and income stay predictable.
  • Refund actions are available from transaction shortcuts and detail screens.

Insights + Scenarios: Compare, Filter, Explain

Charts and scenario switching work together so customers can see both the current result and the planning context behind it.

  • Review overview, map, trend, and pie perspectives from the same statistics area.
  • Use scenarios to compare alternate plans without rebuilding your structure.
  • Keep customer-facing visuals focused on drift, allocation, and cashflow movement.

Flows + Widgets: Faster Daily Execution

Reusable flows reduce repeated input, and widgets keep important actions and summaries visible before the app is opened.

  • Create and manage flow templates for repeated actions.
  • Launch quick-entry paths from the app and the home screen.
  • Pair flows with widgets to shorten the gap between planning and execution.

Data + Investing: Bring More of Your Finance Stack In

The website now shows the current import, export, and investment views instead of the older 2.0 poster images.

  • Import and export workflows keep data portable.
  • Investment screens cover overview, data source, deposit or withdraw, and buy or sell actions.
  • These screenshots now match the app tips catalog instead of the older marketing set.

Privacy

Your data stays with you — stored locally or in iCloud.

Effective Date: 2025-08-03

1. Information We Collect

  • Transaction data for core features.
  • Location (optional) for tagging spending places.
  • Icons you choose (emoji, SF Symbols, custom images).
  • iCloud sync (optional) via your own iCloud account.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve app features.
  • To keep data consistent across devices.
  • No ads — never used for marketing.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or share your data. Disclosure only when legally required.

4. Storage & Security

Data is stored locally or in iCloud (optional), protected by Apple system security.
