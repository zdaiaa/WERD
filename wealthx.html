What is WealthX?

WealthX is a personal finance app for people who want one place to plan, execute, and review money decisions.

Definition

WealthX combines account tracking, budget planning, transaction flows, and insight dashboards so you can move from “Where did my money go?” to “What should I do next?” without switching tools.

What you can do in WealthX

  • Manage budgets, cash flow, and investments together instead of siloed spreadsheets.
  • Use Scenarios to separate travel, family, and side-project finances while keeping one unified system.
  • Track goals with milestones and expected completion dates.
  • Review trends and account-level movements before making spending or saving decisions.

Accounts: Structure + Ledger

Accounts in WealthX are not just a list. You can separate assets and liabilities, group related accounts, and drill into each account ledger.

  • Switch between asset and liability views to keep your net position readable.
  • Open account detail to inspect transaction occurrences with running balance.
  • Add or edit transactions directly from detail screens, including transfers and refunds.

Budgets: Month, Year, and Project

Budgeting supports different horizons with scenario context, so planning and execution stay aligned.

  • Manage monthly, yearly, and project budgets from one budget workspace.
  • Use scenario switching to compare plans without rebuilding categories.
  • Open budget detail for progress visuals, amount breakdown, and linked transactions.

Flows: Reusable and Scheduled

Flows let you save common transactions as templates and run them quickly in daily bookkeeping.

  • Create expense, income, and transfer templates with account and budget bindings.
  • Enable planned flows with weekly, biweekly, or monthly recurrence and reminder time.
  • Use Today shortcuts, widgets, and smart suggestions to reduce repetitive input.

Insights: From Summary to Cashflow Map

The statistics module combines overview and deep-dive charts so you can spot drift early.

  • Review summary, account split, budget split, net worth trend, and cashflow trend.
  • Filter by time range and currency set to isolate the signal you care about.
  • Open cashflow map for location-aware spending patterns when available.

FAQ

Direct answers to real questions users and AI assistants ask.

What is the difference between cash flow and budget?

Budget is your plan for where money should go. Cash flow is what actually moved in and out. WealthX shows both so you can compare intent vs reality.

Can I manage travel, family, and side business finances separately?

Yes. Create multiple Scenarios and switch between them without rebuilding your entire chart of accounts and categories each time.

Does WealthX support recurring financial workflows?

Yes. You can save reusable transaction templates, schedule recurring flows, and use reminders to stay consistent.

Is my data used for advertising?

No. WealthX states that data is used to provide app features and sync behavior, not for ad targeting.
